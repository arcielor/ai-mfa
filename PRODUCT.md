# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students or testers evaluating AI/ML security concepts.

## Product Purpose
Provide a functional login interface alongside visible attack logs to evaluate and demonstrate AI-driven threat detection and mitigation in real-time.

## Brand Personality
Secure, technical, and analytical. Emphasizing the underlying ML intelligence.

## Anti-references
Generic Bootstrap or Material default templates. The design should not feel like a standard off-the-shelf theme.

## Design Principles
1. **Intelligence on display**: Make the ML analysis and risk assessment visible and understandable, not a hidden black box.
2. **Precision over decoration**: Prioritize the clarity of attack logs and functional flows over unnecessary visual flair.
3. **Purpose-built security**: The interface should feel bespoke and technically rigorous, avoiding generic patterns.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Baseline usability with standard semantic HTML. No specific advanced requirements beyond general best practices.
